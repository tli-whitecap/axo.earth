What is Axo Fight Club?

We are a unique NFT project building an environmentally conscious community of Champs, focused on raising money for the preservation of the Axolotl’s wild habitat. The Axo Fight Club is made up of 3,333 Axolotls who have joined forces to escape the pet store, and save their habitat!  Each Axo is unique, and has their own look and personality. Our community celebrates uncommon strength through diversity, creativity, togetherness and the fundamental will to survive!

Join the Fight

We are calling on you to join the Axo Fight Club, and help the Axolotls fight for their future! We have partnered with the Laboratoria de Restauracion Ecologica (LRE) at UNAM University to donate 25% of net proceeds to Axolotl habitat preservation and conservation. This money will go towards the maintenance and expansion of Chinampas, a traditional Aztec farming method which is highly sustainable, and creates the perfect Axolotl habitat. In addition to event access, community governance, and metaverse experiences, Axo buyers will adopt an Axolotl, receiving updates on its health and images of their Axolotl periodically from LRE.
