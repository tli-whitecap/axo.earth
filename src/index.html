A FIGHT FOR THE AXO

The Axo’s habitat has been overtaken. Help fight for their existence.

Axolotls are an endangered species native to Mexico City.

These creatures have the ability to regenerate lost limbs and organs. The preservation of these amphibians are crucial and further research could help unlock the key to human stem cell regeneration.

How to Get Involved

We’re calling you to join the Axo community and help Axolotls in the fight for their future. 25% of proceeds will be donated to the Axolotl foundation and conservation efforts.
